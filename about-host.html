Spacepool
for Hosts

Spacepool is a marketplace that connects workspace operators with the new generation of start-ups, scale-ups and established businesses.

We provide a service that will expose your space to a thriving community who are looking for flexible offices and workspaces from which to operate.

Sign Up as a Host
ON YOUR TERMS
As a Spacepool Host, you have the opportunity to list and hire out your workspace at your convenience. You’re in full control: availability, prices and house rules are up to you, giving you the ability to maximise your return and improve occupancy.

Our technology is secure and fully mobile-optimised, making it simple for you and your guests to list, search and hire workspaces on the go.

Spacepool will expose your brand to new businesses and help you grow your network with our dynamic marketplace.

BENEFITS

Why list with Spacepool?

Reach A Wide Audience

With a variety of businesses looking for a base of operations, Spacepool lets them know that your workspace is available to hire. It’s absolutely free to list on Spacepool and our website connects you with our growing membership, allowing you to showcase the features unique to your listing. Our booking process is designed to be simple and easy to use and our interface matches your workspace with the appropriate Guest, while always ensuring they comply with your high standards.

You’re In Control

It’s your workspace, so you decide on the price, availability, house rules, and cancelation policy. We give you the freedom to accept or deny any hire requests, and with the ability to post multiple listings, you can add or remove them as you wish. You can set bespoke terms with your Guest; our online messaging service lets you contact them directly to discuss any details or even agree one-off deals.

Convenience

Our listing process is easy and straightforward, taking only a matter of minutes. We take all the admin off your hands and the Spacepool service means everything is online and paper-free. As well as the facility for on-the-go bookings and listings, we take care of the payment process, so whether you want to confirm, reject or find out a little more, all you need to do is decide who you want to hire to.

Hassle-free Payment

We handle payment on your behalf, taking the full amount from your Guest once the hire request is accepted. There’s no need to worry about invoices or unpaid fees, and once we receive the money we transfer the balance to your nominated account, less our fee. At Spacepool we use the industry best practice for dealing with your money via our trusted payment partner, who ensure there are no missed, incorrect or lost payments, as well as setting strict data protection standards to protect your debit/credit card details. You will have the option to download any invoices from your Spacepool account.

    Sign-up
